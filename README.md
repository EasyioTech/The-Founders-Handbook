# The Founders Handbook

**Your Complete Guide to Building a Startup in India**

## What is The Founders Handbook?

The Founders Handbook is a comprehensive online resource designed specifically for Indian entrepreneurs and startup founders. Whether you're just starting with an idea or preparing for your Series A funding round, this handbook provides everything you need to know about building a successful startup in India.
