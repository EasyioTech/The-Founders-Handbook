# PREVENTION OF SEXUAL HARASSMENT (POSH) POLICY

**Effective Date**: [Date]
**Applicability**: All Employees, Interns, and Consultants of [Company Name].

## 1. COMMITMENT
[Company Name] is committed to providing a safe working environment free from sexual harassment and discrimination for all employees. We have zero tolerance for sexual harassment.

## 2. SCOPE
This policy adheres to the **Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013**.

## 3. DEFINITION OF SEXUAL HARASSMENT
Sexual harassment includes any unwelcome act or behavior (whether directly or by implication) such as:
1.  Physical contact and advances.
2.  A demand or request for sexual favors.
3.  Making sexually colored remarks.
4.  Showing pornography.
5.  Any other unwelcome physical, verbal, or non-verbal conduct of sexual nature.

## 4. INTERNAL COMPLAINTS COMMITTEE (ICC)
(Mandatory for companies with >10 employees)
The Company has constituted an ICC to handle complaints:
*   **Presiding Officer**: [Senior Female Employee Name]
*   **Member**: [Employee Name]
*   **Member**: [Employee Name]
*   **External Member**: [NGO/Lawyer Name]

## 5. COMPLAINT MECHANISM
*   Any aggrieved woman may make a complaint in writing to the ICC within **3 months** of the incident.
*   Email for complaints: **posh@[company.com]**.

## 6. INQUIRY PROCESS
1.  ICC will send a copy of the complaint to the Respondent within 7 days.
2.  ICC will investigate the matter confidentially (principles of natural justice).
3.  Inquiry to be completed within 90 days.

## 7. DISCIPLINARY ACTION
If the allegation is proved, action may include:
*   Written apology.
*   Withholding promotion/increments.
*   Termination of employment.
*   Reporting to the police (for criminal offenses).
