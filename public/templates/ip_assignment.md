# INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY (IP) ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT

**THIS AGREEMENT** is made on [Date]

**BETWEEN:**
1.  **[Company Name]**, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, with its registered office at [Address] (the "**Company**"); and
2.  **[Assignor Name]** (Employee/Consultant/Founder), residing at [Address] (the "**Assignor**").

## 1. ASSIGNMENT
The Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to any and all "Intellectual Property" created, developed, or conceived by the Assignor:
(a) During the course of their engagement/employment with the Company;
(b) Using the Company’s time, resources, or facilities; or
(c) Related to the Company’s current or anticipated business.

**"Intellectual Property"** includes but is not limited to:
*   Software code (source and object), algorithms, and APIs.
*   Designs, UI/UX, graphics, and wireframes.
*   Business plans, customer lists, and marketing strategies.
*   Inventions, patents, trademarks, and copyrights.

## 2. WAIVER OF MORAL RIGHTS
The Assignor retains no "moral rights" in the IP and waives any right to be identified as the author or to object to any modification of the work by the Company.

## 3. FURTHER ASSURANCES
The Assignor agrees to sign any additional documents (such as patent applications) reasonably requested by the Company to perfect the Company’s ownership of the IP, even after the termination of the engagement.

The Assignor represents that they have listed below all IP created prior to this agreement that belongs to them and is NOT assigned to the Company (if none, write "None").
*   [List Prior Inventions or 'None']

## 5. CONSIDERATION
The Assignor acknowledges that their salary/fees and continued engagement with the Company constitute sufficient consideration for this assignment.
