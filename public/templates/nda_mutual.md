# MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (NDA)

**This Agreement** is valid as of [Date].

**BETWEEN**:
1.  **[Party A Name]** (e.g., The Company)
2.  **[Party B Name]** (e.g., The Vendor/Partner)

(Collectively the "Parties")

## 1. PURPOSE
The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship ("Purpose") and will need to share Confidential Information with each other.

## 2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
"Confidential Information" means all non-public information disclosed by one party ("Discloser") to the other ("Recipient"), whether orally or in writing, including:
*   Business plans, financial data, customer lists.
*   Software source code, technical specs, algorithms.
*   Trade secrets and marketing strategies.

## 3. OBLIGATIONS
The Recipient agrees to:
*   Use the Confidential Information **only** for the Purpose.
*   Protect the information with the same degree of care as their own confidential data.
*   Not disclose the information to any third party (except employees who "need to know" and are bound by confidentiality).

## 4. EXCEPTIONS
Confidential Information does not include information that:
*   Is or becomes public knowledge through no fault of the Recipient.
*   Was already known to the Recipient prior to disclosure.
*   Is independently developed by the Recipient without using the Discloser's data.
*   Is required to be disclosed by law (with prior notice to the Discloser).

## 5. TERM
This Agreement covers information disclosed for a period of [1] year from the date hereof. The duty of confidentiality survives for **[3-5] years** after disclosure (or indefinitely for trade secrets).
