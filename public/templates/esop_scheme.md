# EMPLOYEE STOCK OPTION PLAN (ESOP) 2025 - SCHEME DRAFT

**NAME**: [COMPANY NAME] EMPLOYEE STOCK OPTION PLAN 2025

## 1. OBJECTIVE
The objective of this ESOP Scheme is to attract, retain, and motivate specialized talent by sharing wealth created by the Company.

## 2. TOTAL POOL
The Plan creates a pool of **[Number of Options]** options, representing approximately **[10-15]%** of the Company's fully diluted equity. Each option converts into one Equity Share upon exercise.

## 3. GRANT OF OPTIONS
*   The Grant is made by the Board (or Compensation Committee) via a **Grant Letter**.
*   The Grant Letter specifies the number of options, Vesting Start Date, and Exercise Price.

## 4. VESTING SCHEDULE
Unless otherwise specified in the Grant Letter, the standard vesting schedule is:
*   **Cliff**: 1 Year from Grant Date (0% vests).
*   **Vesting**:
    *   **End of Year 1**: 25%
    *   **End of Year 2**: 25% (Monthly/Quarterly thereafter)
    *   **End of Year 3**: 25%
    *   **End of Year 4**: 25%

## 5. EXERCISE PRICE

*   *Alternatively*: FMV (Fair Market Value) at the time of grant for later stages.

## 6. EXERCISE PERIOD
*   **While Employed**: Vested options can be exercised at any time.
*   **Upon Resignation**: Employee must exercise vested options within **[30-90] days** of the last working day. Unexercised options lapse.
*   **Upon Termination for Cause**: All options (vested and unvested) lapse immediately.

## 7. LIQUIDITY EVENT (EXIT)
In case of a confusing/acquisition (Exit), all unvested options may be accelerated (vested immediately) at the discretion of the Board, allowing employees to participate in the sale.
