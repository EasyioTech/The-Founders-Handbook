## 1. BACKGROUND
The Founders have collaborated to key concepts, business plans, and intellectual property regarding [Brief Business Description] (the "Project"). This Agreement sets out the understanding between the Founders regarding the Company.

## 2. EQUITY SPLIT
The initial equity ownership of the Company shall be split as follows:
*   **[Founder 1 Name]**: [XX]%
*   **[Founder 2 Name]**: [XX]%

## 3. VESTING SCHEDULE (Standard/Best Practice)
To ensure long-term commitment, the Founders' shares shall be subject to a **Reverse Vesting** schedule:
*   **Vestng Period**: 4 Years (48 Months).
*   **Cliff Period**: 1 Year (12 Months).
    *   If a Founder leaves before 12 months, they retain 0% of their shares.
    *   At month 12, 25% of their shares vest immediately.
    *   Remaining 75% vests monthly (1/48th per month) over the next 36 months.

## 4. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
*   **[Founder 1 Name]**: [CEO/CTO/etc]. Responsible for: [Key Responsibilities].
*   **[Founder 2 Name]**: [CEO/CTO/etc]. Responsible for: [Key Responsibilities].

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY (IP) ASSIGNMENT
Each Founder assigns to the Company all rights, title, and interest in and to any and all Intellectual Property (code, designs, business plans, domain names) created by them related to the business of the Company, whether created before or after the date of this Agreement.
*   This assignment is absolute and irrevocable.
*   Founders waive all "moral rights" in favor of the Company.

## 6. DECISION MAKING
*   **Day-to-Day Decisions**: Made by the CEO or respective functional lead.
*   **Major Decisions**: Require unanimous consent (or majority vote) of Founders holding > [50]% equity. Major decisions include:
    *   Selling the company.
    *   Raising investment / Diluting shares.
    *   Changing the core business direction (Pivot).
    *   Taking on debt > [Amount].

## 7. DEPARTURE / EXIT
If a Founder leaves the Company (voluntarily or terminated for cause):
*   **Unvested Shares**: Repurchased by the Company at nominal value (e.g., Face Value).
*   **Vested Shares**: 
    *   *Bad Leaver* (Fraud, Criminal Act): Company may repurchase vested shares at [Fair Market Value / Discount].
    *   *Good Leaver* (Death, Disability, Mutual Agreement): Founder retains vested shares.

## 8. NON-COMPETE & NON-SOLICIT
*   Founders agree not to work for or start a competing business for the duration of their employment and for [12-24] months after leaving.
*   Founders agree not to poach Company employees or clients for [12-24] months after leaving.

## 9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
In case of a deadlock:
1.  Good faith negotiation for 30 days.
2.  Mediation by a neutral third party advisor approved by both.
3.  Arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 (India). Seat: [City].
